Why Come to Oxford

Here at Oxford, there are a lot of fun things to do. The class catalogue here has a lot of classes for different interests. There are electives for math, science, languages, social studies, technology, medicine, and many other subjects. There are also a lot of different after-school activities that you can do here as well, with a lot of athletic programs, a robotics team, and after-school groups for some classes.
